Earthquake Safety Education

Before an Earthquake

  • Create an emergency plan with your family
  • Identify safe spots in each room
  • Secure heavy furniture and appliances
  • Keep emergency supplies ready
  • Know how to turn off gas, water, and electricity

During an Earthquake

  • Drop: Get down on hands and knees
  • Cover: Take cover under a desk/table
  • Hold On: Hold onto shelter and protect head
  • Stay where you are until shaking stops
  • If outdoors, move away from buildings

After an Earthquake

  • Check for injuries and provide first aid
  • Check for hazards (gas leaks, electrical damage)
  • Use flashlights, not candles
  • Stay out of damaged buildings
  • Listen to emergency broadcasts

Emergency Kit Essentials

  • Water (1 gallon per person per day)
  • Non-perishable food (3+ days)
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • First aid kit and medications
  • Whistle, radio, phone chargers
